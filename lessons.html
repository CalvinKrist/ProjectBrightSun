Lesson 0: Installing Prerequisite Software

Three programs need to be installed: VirtualBox, Vagrant, and Packer. Go and download the version of each that is appropriate for your system.

VirtualBox and Vagrant can be easily installed by running their installers and restarting your system. Packer is a little more complicated. On Windows, place 'packer.exe' in a folder on "C:\Program Files". You then need to go to your advanced system settings by right clicking 'MyPC', selecting 'Properties' and going to 'Advanced System Settings' on the left. Click 'Environment Variables' in the bottom right and then open the 'Path'. Add a new variable to the path pointing to the directory packer.exe was placed in.

All of the required software should now be installed!
